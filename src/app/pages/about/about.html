IV Conferencia de Profesores de Inglés 2019

Date Location Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica

The Costa Rica Institute of Technology and its Language Science School have worried over the years about the training of teachers of English in our country. One of our purposes is to bring teachers together so that they can share their experiences in the different fields related to teaching, from activities that are successful in the classroom to the latest in pedagogy, the use of technology in teaching and learning, methodology, professional development, research related to English teaching and others. With this purpose in mind, we have organized the IV Conference of Teachers of English TEC 2019. The conference’s main theme is Challenges and Opportunities in 21st Century English Teaching and we will have presentations with experts from Costa Rica and other countries who will share their knowledge and experience in the field of English teaching.
